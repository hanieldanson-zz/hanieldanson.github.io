Banjo Burning Bob vs. Bela the Banjo Player

Banjo Burning Bob, if you couldn't tell by his name, abhors banjos and is on the hunt for any banjos that he can find and burn in the country. Bela, the hero in our story, is a mean banjo player tasked with saving the banjos from imminent destruction. The story starts off with Banjo Burning Bob hunting across the country for banjos. Bela is relaxing at home until a local stops by to tell him that Banjo Burning Bob is on the hunt. Bela grabs his magic banjo and looks for his fingerpicks before heading out to search for Banjo Burning Bob. Unfortunately, Bela doesn't have the best memory and forgets his fingerpicks more often than not. If Bela remembers the his banjo fingerpicks, which he needs to play his magic banjo properly, then he automatically wins the first round against Banjo Burning Bob. As much as Banjo Burning Bob hates banjos, even he can't help but fall into a trance when Bela plays that magic banjo. If Bela forgets his fingerpicks, then all bets are off and Banjo Burning Bob is going to torch some banjos. You are: Bela Goals: Prevent Banjo Burning Bob from reaching 0 banjos, which means he burned all of the banjos Get rewarded with one banjo every time you defeat Banjo Burning Bob Reach a total number of 20 banjos to win the game
